About Me

Previous Air Force Medical Assistant trained to be a Full Stack Web Developer at Flatiron School. I have a passion for learning problem-solving. I have experience in Ruby on Rails and JavaScript-based programming and a background in the medical field. Exposure as a pre-teen to programming, education in Computer Information Systems, and increasing systems use in patient care led to an interest in web development. I bring strong skills in problem solving and teamwork that help companies build web applications to improve the way we live. I'm excited for challenging work, continuous growth, and learning more programming languages and techniques through individual and team projects. Currently, I work part-time as an adjunct instructor for a college online teaching medical terminology as a foundation course for students going into medical fields. I have also signed up with socialcoder.org to get experience programming on a team while looking for a job.
